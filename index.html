Our Recipies

From a healthy lunch to evening snacks to a hearty dinne, FOOD Network provides you with the means to satisfy your cravings throughout the day. Sit back and relax - let foodpanda Bangladesh take the pressure off your shoulders.

Sorsebata Ilish

Hilsa' fish is the highlight of the local cuisine. The fish is marinated with turmeric and delicately simmered in a mustard-poppy seed paste along with the five-spice mix.

Bangali Lumb

Lamb marinated in the comforting flavour of yogurt and cooked in mustard oil along with mustard seeds, almonds and castor sugar that adds a sweet undertone.

Doi Maach

Doi machch is a comforting fish curry. Tender chunks of fish are cooked in the soothing flavor of yogurt along with mild spices. It tastes best when served with plain rice.
